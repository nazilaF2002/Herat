Parwaneh
Parwaneh is one of the most popular recreational areas of Herat province. It is located in the north of Herat. It is a mountainous area where people go to parties. I took this picture on 3/17/2023.

Takhte Sefar
Takhte Safar is one of the biggest entertainment places in Herat province. It is located in the north of the city. It has a forest and a big game city.

Small Island
The small island is also one of the recreational places of Herat city. It is located at the Pashtun bridge of Herat, which has a green space and a small island in the middle of the water. It also has small boats for sailing. Its image is very attractive from the night side.

Shidaei
Shidaei Herat is the largest recreational forest, it has a green and peaceful atmosphere, it is located in the western part of the city, and it is one of the most popular recreational places of the people of Herat.
